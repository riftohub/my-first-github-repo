Parts Of Speech !

Parts of Speech - are some basic categories of words , based on how there used in sentences .

There are 8 types of parts of speech :

1. Noun
2. Pronoun
3. Verb
4. Adjective
5. Adverb
6. Preposition
7. Conjunction
8. Interjection

Noun (name of a person , place , thing , idea or feelings )

There are 7 types of Noun

1. Common Noun
2. Proper Noun
3. Concrete Noun
4. Abstract Noun
5. Countable Noun
6. Uncountable Noun
7. Collective Noun

Pronoun (that replaces noun) :

There are 7 types of pronoun

1. Personal Pronoun (replaces subject & object) - I , He, She , It, We , Us, Them , They, Him, Her
2. Possessive Pronoun (Show Ownership) - Mine, Our, Theirs, hers, its, His
3. Reflexive Pronoun (Show your self) - myself, itself, yourself, himself, themselves,herself
4. Demonstrative Pronoun (showing something) - This, That, These , Those
5. Interrogative Pronoun (question) - Who? , Which ? , Where ? , What ?
6. Relative Pronoun (bla bla bla) - Who , which , what, where
7. Indefinite Pronoun (Not showing Specific) - someone,somebody, anyone, no one, everyone, something , anything , nothing, everything
